- Core Web Vitals (LCP, FID, CLS, FCP, TTFB)

CUSTOM EVENTS:

- trackSearch(term, filters)

SETUP:

- Monitor performance issues
- Data-driven decisions
- Identify popular properties

10. DEPLOYMENT CHECKLIST
================================================================================
